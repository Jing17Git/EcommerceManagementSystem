# ⚡ 5-Minute Quick Guide: Update Payment Accounts

## 🎯 What You Need
- Admin login credentials
- Your GCash number
- Your PayMaya number (optional)
- Your Bank account number (optional)

---

## 🚀 Quick Steps (5 Minutes)

### ⏱️ Minute 1: Login

```
1. Open: http://localhost:8000/admin/payment-methods
2. Login with admin account
```

### ⏱️ Minute 2: Update GCash

```
1. Click "Edit" on GCash row
2. Change:
   Account Name: [Your Name]
   Account Number: [Your GCash Number]
3. Click "Update Payment Method"
```

### ⏱️ Minute 3: Update PayMaya

```
1. Click "Edit" on PayMaya row
2. Change:
   Account Name: [Your Name]
   Account Number: [Your PayMaya Number]
3. Click "Update Payment Method"
```

### ⏱️ Minute 4: Update Bank Accounts

```
1. Click "Edit" on Bank Transfer - BDO
2. Change:
   Account Name: [Your Name]
   Account Number: [Your BDO Account]
3. Click "Update Payment Method"

4. Repeat for BPI (or delete if you don't have)
```

### ⏱️ Minute 5: Test

```
1. Logout from admin
2. Login as buyer
3. Add item to cart
4. Go to checkout
5. Select GCash
6. Verify YOUR account details appear ✓
```

---

## ✅ Done!

Your payment accounts are now updated!

Buyers will now see YOUR account details when they checkout.
